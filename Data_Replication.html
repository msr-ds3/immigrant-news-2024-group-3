Extension

We decided to examine this claim in the paper: While there was a significant increase in negative coverage of immigrants after Trumps inauguration, the Trump administration also engaged in substantial anti-immigrant messaging and policy initiatives To do this, we are looking at the association between media coverage of the border crisis and data of how many people were apprehended or expelled at the Mexican border.
